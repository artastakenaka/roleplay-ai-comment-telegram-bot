Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

We (Roleplay AI Bot) take your privacy seriously.

1. What we collect

  • Telegram bot token (to operate your bot)
  • Your custom AI persona prompt / description
  • Telegram chat IDs of channels/groups you connect
  • Messages you forward during setup (only once, to verify connection)
  • Usage statistics (number of comments/replies generated — no message content stored)

2. What we do NOT collect / store

  • Content of messages in your groups/channels after setup
  • Personal messages of your users
  • Any payment information (handled by external provider)

3. Data usage

Data is used only to:

  • Run your AI bot
  • Show you usage statistics in your dashboard
  • Provide support when you contact us

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your data with third parties except:

  • AI inference provider (xAI / Grok API) — only sends messages that need a reply/comment
  • When required by law

5. Data deletion

You can delete your bot data at any time by sending /delete_my_data to @roleplay_ai_bot. All your settings, prompts and connections will be permanently removed.

Questions? → contact us directly in Telegram.
